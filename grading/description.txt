Grading Scaner

This app scans student's hand-written answer sheets  and teacher's standard answer sheets of quizzes，
uses artificial intelligence to recognize format, words and digits, and perform automatic grading.

这个应用程序能扫描学生的手写的测验答题卡和老师的标准答案卡的图像，用人工智能识别格式、文字和数字并自动评分。


Some schools have policies prohibiting students from using PC and mobile device in school. To speed up the process of grading paper submission of quizzes,  teacher user can use this app to scan submitted paper answer sheets to perform automatic grading. This app uses artificial intelligence to analyze the images of student's hand-written answer sheets  or standard answer sheets, and recognize columns and rows  to generate  data grids. Then for each cell in a grid, it uses three typies of machine learning routines to recognize  hand-written words and digits. It performs automatic grading once a standard answer sheet is scanned. It also manages the generated database of quizzes, including searching records and sending reports.
